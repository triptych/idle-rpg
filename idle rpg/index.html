How to Play

  • Click on your player name to change it.
  • Click on your avatar to change it.
  • You will gain experience points (XP) automatically over time.
  • As you gain XP, your level will increase.
  • You will receive random items in your inventory every minute.
  • Every hour, you will move to a new location.
  • Monsters will appear randomly, and you can fight them to gain XP and items.
